Is your sink faucet clogged?

Sinks can get clogged with calcium build-up and debris from broken pipes. Every faucet in a sink has a little screw-on piece that has a filter inside the piece. You should unscrew the piece and check for debris. If debris are present, then carefuly remove the mesh and clean it out. Remember how the whole piece was put together. Then put the mesh inside of the piece and screw it back on the faucet. If that does not fix the problem, then it is a pressure issue.
