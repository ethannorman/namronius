Crafting

Simple Braid

For this project you are going to need:

  • Yarn (any color)
  • Scissors
  • A clip board

Take the yarn and cut it into three equal lengths. Line up the three strands and tie a knot at the end of the 3 strands put together. Then take the knotted end of the new strand and clip it to the clip board. Then take spread out each strand like a fan. Take the left strand and put it over the middle strand. Then take the middle strand and put it over to the left side. Take the new middle strand and put it over the right strand. Take the right strand and put it over to the middle. Repeat this process until you run out of string. You can do this with more than 3 strands of string.
